About Serena

Creating at the intersection of emotion, memory, and color.

Serena is a visual artist based in Brooklyn, New York, whose work explores the quiet spaces between perception and feeling. Drawing from themes of nature, identity, and the passage of time, her pieces invite viewers into layered worlds of texture and light.

With a background in fine art and digital media, Serena works across oil painting, mixed media, and digital illustration. Her work has been exhibited in galleries across New York, Los Angeles, and London, and is held in private collections worldwide.

When she's not in the studio, Serena teaches community workshops on intuitive painting and collaborates with fellow artists on immersive installation projects.
